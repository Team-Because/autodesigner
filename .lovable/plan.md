

## BrandTonic Studio — Brand Setup Guideline

A universal template you can share with anyone setting up their brand. Fill in each section, then paste into the corresponding fields in BrandTonic Studio.

---

### HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

Fill in each section below for your brand. The system has 4 input areas — this guide maps directly to them:

1. **Brand Brief / Guidelines** → Sections A through F below
2. **Color Palette** → Section G (entered via color pickers)
3. **Tone & Target Audience** → Section D (paste into "Tone & Audience" field)
4. **The "Never" List** → Section F (paste into "Never List" field)
5. **Brand Assets** → Section H (uploaded as images, tagged by type)

---

### SECTION A — BRAND IDENTITY

- **Brand Name:** [Full official name as it should appear on creatives]
- **One-Liner:** [Single sentence — what you are and who you serve]
  - *Example: "Skill-focused, modern learning made accessible and future-ready."*
- **What You Do (3–5 bullets):**
  - [Core offering #1]
  - [Core offering #2]
  - [Core offering #3]
- **Differentiators (what sets you apart):**
  - [Unique strength #1]
  - [Unique strength #2]

### SECTION B — MANDATORY ELEMENTS

Things that MUST appear on every creative. List each with its exact text.

- **Brand Name (as displayed):** [e.g., "SWS SHIVASHISH WORLD SCHOOL"]
- **Tagline / Slogan:** [e.g., "Future-Ready Learning" — leave blank if none]
- **Default CTA:** [e.g., "Admissions Open | Enrol Now | Apply Now"]
- **Contact Information:** [e.g., "+91 98xxx | www.example.com"]
- **Legal / Compliance Text:** [e.g., "CBSE Affiliated | RERA No. XXXXX"]
- **Other Mandatory Text:** [Certifications, hashtags, etc.]

---

### SECTION C — BRAND VOICE & MESSAGING

- **Voice Traits (3–6 adjectives):** [e.g., Modern, Trustworthy, Clear, Aspirational, Warm]
- **Messaging Pillars (3–5 recurring themes):**
  1. [e.g., "Admissions Open — a confident first step toward a brighter future"]
  2. [e.g., "Skill-focused learning that connects to real outcomes"]
  3. [...]
- **Words / Phrases TO USE:** [e.g., Admissions Open, Future-ready, Growth, Pathway]
- **Words / Phrases TO AVOID:** [e.g., Guaranteed results, No.1 in the world, Last chance]

### SECTION D — TARGET AUDIENCE

- **Primary Audience:** [Who, age, demographics, psychographics]
- **Secondary Audience:** [If any]
- **Desired Emotional Response:** [How should the viewer FEEL?]
  - *Example: "Confident, inspired, reassured about their child's future"*

### SECTION E — VISUAL STYLE & RULES

- **Mood:** [e.g., Clean, modern, aspirational]
- **Lighting:** [e.g., Warm natural light, bright and airy]
- **Photography:** [e.g., Real people, candid moments, studio product shots]
- **Layout:** [e.g., Minimal with generous whitespace, editorial typography]
- **Textures:** [e.g., Subtle gradients allowed, no heavy patterns]
- **Composition Rules:**
  - [e.g., "Headline hierarchy: Main headline > Support line > CTA"]
  - [e.g., "Keep whitespace; avoid heavy borders"]

---

### SECTION F — DO'S AND DON'TS

Split into two categories so the AI handles visual and content rules separately.

**VISUAL DO'S:**
- [e.g., "Use warm, natural lighting in all scenes"]
- [e.g., "Maintain premium, minimal composition"]

**VISUAL DON'TS:**
- [e.g., "No generic crests, emblems, or shield logos"]
- [e.g., "No overcrowded layouts or neon colors"]
- [e.g., "No blurry text or low-resolution images"]

**CONTENT DO'S:**
- [e.g., "Always include grade range and academic year"]
- [e.g., "Use approved CTAs only"]

**CONTENT DON'TS:**
- [e.g., "Never use fear-based urgency (hurry / last chance)"]
- [e.g., "Never make exaggerated claims (100% placement)"]

### SECTION H — BRAND ASSETS (IMAGES TO UPLOAD)

For each image, provide:

| File | Tag | Usage Rule |
|------|-----|-----------|
| [e.g., logo.png] | Logo | "Place top-left, 6-8% clear space. Never recolor or redraw." |
| [e.g., hero.jpg] | Hero Image | "Use as mood setter. Creative freedom on crop." |
| [e.g., product.png] | Product | "Show as-is, studio lighting, preserve proportions." |

**Available Tags:** Logo, Hero Image, Architecture, Lifestyle, Masterplan, Product, Mascot, Pattern/Texture, Icon, Other

**Asset Rules (apply to all):**
- [e.g., "Logo must never be stretched or placed on busy backgrounds"]
- [e.g., "Product photos must maintain original proportions"]

---

### HOW TO PASTE INTO BRANDTONIC STUDIO

| Studio Field | What to paste |
|-------------|--------------|
| **Brand Brief / Guidelines** | Sections A, B, C, E, and DO'S from F. Use `## HEADERS` for structure. |
| **Tone & Target Audience** | Section D + voice traits from C |
| **The "Never" List** | DON'TS from Section F. Use `## VISUAL NEVERS` and `## CONTENT NEVERS` headers. |
| **Color Palette** | Enter via color pickers + "Add Color" button |
| **Brand Assets** | Upload images, select tag from dropdown |

---

**Tip:** The more specific and structured your input, the better the AI output. Vague instructions = inconsistent creatives. Precise rules = on-brand results every time.
